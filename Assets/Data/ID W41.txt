Author: Alicia Smith
Subject: Our Quiet Home

To my love,

I hope the universe has been kind and you have been safe. Thomas has been studying harder even though he’s still so young. But just between you and me, I can’t help but feel like something is off about him. He always looks like he is deep in thought, or not as happy as before. I wonder if he is thinking about something, it would be nice if he could talk to me about it. Don’t worry, I’ll make sure I relay the information to you as well.

The year of just living alone at home hasn’t always been smooth sailing, there were moments where I felt like you hadn’t left the Earth and were here to keep me company. Don’t get me wrong though, I am very proud of the both of you for pursuing what you both love to do, but sometimes being emotional is a part of being human isn’t it? Our home has fallen completely silent since Tom became quieter, save for the few weekends my friends would come over to visit and keep me company or ask me out for lunch or dinner. It has left me reflecting on the life we’ve had together up till this point, and despite the circumstances, I could not have asked for a better husband, family and friends to be surrounded by in this lonely time. 

I will always remember you whenever I look at the stars in the sky, and I hope somewhere out there, you’re looking out to remember all of us too.

Forever yours,
Alicia
