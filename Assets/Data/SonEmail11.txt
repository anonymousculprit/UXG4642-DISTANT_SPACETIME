Author: Thomas Smith
Subject: You disappoint me.

I don’t even know why I bother writing anymore at this point. Just seeing anything resembling you pisses me off so much. But the least I can do for mom now is to say this to you:

I despise you at every single moment of my life. You weren’t the one who had to see how lonely her eyes looked during her final moments. You weren’t the one who had to arrange her funeral. You weren’t the one who was there during her burial. It’s ridiculous. Why did you do this to her? If this was your dream, why drag mom into it? Why make her go through all this pain? You clearly don’t love her more than whatever the outer void universe can provide you. 

I hate you, but more than anything, I hate the outer space that took you away from us. 

Goodbye.
