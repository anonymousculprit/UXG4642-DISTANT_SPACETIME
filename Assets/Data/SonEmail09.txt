Subject: Hope you read this.

Seriously? After so many years?

I haven’t been able to bring myself to write to you, because I’m afraid that once I open the mailbox, I’m still not going to see a reply from you. I decided to stop writing to you, just to see if you would send me anything first, but I guess some space rocks is more important to you than the family, right? That’s cool to know, I guess. I’m glad I didn’t go down the same path as you, it’s stupid how hopeful I was when I was younger.

How important can the universe be if you are not even by mom’s side during the time that she needs you the most? She keeps telling me that you’ll come back eventually, and not to be hard on you. You know what I think? She’s too damn kind to you, that’s what she is. I’m the one by her bedside when she’s warded in. I hope you can kick yourself back into shape and do your job as her husband by coming back this instance to see her before it’s too late.

This is your last chance.
