Subject: 10 Years...

Dear Dylon Smith,

I’m sure you’ve noticed this by now but... In the span of just yesterday to today, a decade had suddenly passed on Earth. I received the email warning from HQ, but... I didn’t expect for 10 whole years to pass just like that! 

I’ve only just received the email from my husband and… It turns out that he went ahead adopting a child anyway. He told me that my absence has filled him with intense loneliness, so he decided to adopt a child to reduce that feeling. 

On one hand, I am happy that he is taking the steps needed to tend to his emotions, but on the other hand, I can’t help but feel a little betrayed that he had gone ahead with the adoption without me. I know it sounds foolish of me to think that he would wait for any more than a decade to return to Earth, but... I just can’t shake off this pain in my heart. I’m afraid that if I close my eyes today, I will wake up the next day to my loved ones leaving me behind...

Smith, do you think that... going on this mission was a... mistake? Sorry for burdening you with this, but I’m sure you feel the same as well, that’s why I’m writing to you about this. You are the only person who can understand how I feel, right?

Warm Regards,
Mark Hender
