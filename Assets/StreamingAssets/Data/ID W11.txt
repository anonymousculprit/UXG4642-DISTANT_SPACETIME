Subject: Tom’s Birthday

Hello Dear,

I hope your journeys through outer space has been safe! Although the house has gotten quieter than usual since your departure, Thomas and I have been keeping each other company and we are doing just fine! Tom’s 11th birthday is coming up, and I have been thinking of some ideas on what we could do together for the celebrations.

I do recall you telling me you have been assigned to take some pictures, I think we could show them to all our guests if that is possible. It would be a sure way to start conversations and to show how proud we are to have you as a family member who is given this honour and duty from Earth. I believe Tom also wants to invite his friends over and it would be really nice to share with them your experiences in outer space so far. Should you have any stories of it, do feel free to tell us.

Seeing you off would always be an ache etched to my heart, but love is one of the things that we are capable of perceiving that transcends time and space isn’t it? I love you to the ends of the universe and back and may your journeys be safe!

Forever yours,
Alicia
