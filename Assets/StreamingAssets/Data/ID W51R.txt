Date: 03/02/2X41
Author: Alicia Smith
Subject: I love you.

To my dearest husband,

I must apologise for not speaking to you in so long. Life moved quickly for all of us, and I got afraid that, you might not be around anymore, but I must write to you this time, even if it may be my last. I pray this email reaches you, no matter where you are.

It is the hardest thing for me to type this, especially to my husband who is light years away in space, but I must tell you that I have fallen sick recently. Thomas has taken me to the hospital and I am writing this email from my ward while recovering to the best I can. The doctors are trying their best to help me to the best of their abilities, but I was told that I may not have much time left. I haven’t been able to see our son as often as I’d like due to his busy schedule as well as the limited visiting time here at the hospital, but it really has given me the time to think about and appreciate all that has happened in my life.

Even though I may not meet you again here on Earth, I would just like you to remember that as your wife, our hearts and souls belong to each other, no matter how far we may be apart and what dangers or sickness may befall us. Should you ever return, I have left a box in our closet for you, on the top right shelf. I have let Thomas know about this too, but I do hope that the both of you would be able to open the box together one day. I love the both of you with all my heart.

Till we meet again,
Alicia
